Suntory Torys Classic is a light and easy-drinking Japanese spirit. Due to being below 40% abv, this cannot be called a whisky in the EU, but is still made from a blend of malt and grain.

Customer Reviews

Reviews (2)

Reviews

  • 4

    Phil James,
    2 December 2020

    I thought I should give this a try, this Torys classic is not as strong as the usual whisky but that doesn't detract from the flavour. This is more like the 70 proof whisky of old, very easy to drink and a good entry level into the world of Japanese whisky. This is not as sophisticated as the more expensive examples but a very nice easy way to start the experience.

  • 3

    Hiram,
    27 December 2018

    This is not a high level whisky, so do not expect single malt quality for $17 per bottle. For the price it is a very palatable drink. Best cut with water, drinking it neat burns like fire.
